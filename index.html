// MANIFESTO

What We Believe

01

Attention is sacred.

Every notification is a claim on someone's consciousness. We don't send notifications to re-engage you. We don't optimize for time-on-app. If you haven't opened BackRoad in a week, we consider that a success, not a failure.

02

Metrics corrupt.

The moment you see a like count, you start performing for it. The moment you see follower numbers, you start comparing. We removed all visible metrics. Your depth score is private. Your connections are yours. Nobody knows how "popular" you are, including you.

03

Speed kills conversation.

Every post on BackRoad has a 3-hour delay before it becomes visible. This isn't a bug. It's the most important feature. You can't dunk. You can't pile on. You can't react in anger. By the time your words appear, you've had time to mean them.

04

Small rooms, deep talks.

Campfire rooms hold 12 people and last 12 hours, then they're gone. No archives. No screenshots. No performative audience. Just the people in the room, talking like people talk when nobody's recording.

05

Creators deserve 90%.

If you make something and people value it, you should keep almost all of what you earn. 90% to creators. 10% to us. No middlemen. No "creator funds" that mysteriously shrink. Direct. Transparent. Yours.

"The best conversations I've ever had happened in places where nobody was watching."

// ANTI-FEATURES

What We Removed (And Why)

The best features of BackRoad are things we deliberately left out.

We Removed

✗

Visible Like Counts

You can appreciate a post (we call it "warmth"), but nobody sees the number. Not even you. Appreciation without performance.

✗

Follower Counts

You connect with people, not audiences. Your connections are private. No influencer hierarchy. No clout chasing.

✗

Algorithmic Feed

Posts appear chronologically from your connections. No engagement optimization. No rage-bait promotion. No filter bubble.

✗

Infinite Scroll

Your feed has a bottom. When you've seen everything, you're done. No manufactured FOMO. No "you might also like."

✗

Re-engagement Notifications

We never ping you to come back. No "you haven't posted in 3 days." No "trending near you." Your phone is quiet.

We Added

✓

3-Hour Post Delay

Every post marinates for 3 hours before going live. Edit freely during the delay. Say what you mean, not what you feel in the moment.

✓

Depth Scoring

Private score based on conversation quality, not quantity. Thoughtful replies weight more than hot takes. Long-form over soundbites.

✓

Campfire Rooms

Ephemeral 12-person rooms that last 12 hours. Real conversations, not broadcasts. Gone when the fire goes out.

✓

Warmth (Not Likes)

Appreciate posts privately. The creator knows someone cared. Nobody else sees a number. Removes the performance incentive entirely.

✓

Daily Digest

One notification per day, max. A curated summary from your connections. Read it when you're ready. Or don't. We won't follow up.

How Depth Works

Measured, Not Gamed

Depth scoring uses NLP to assess substantive contribution -- references, novel ideas, building on others' points. You can't game it by being verbose. Short and insightful scores higher than long and empty.

Private by Design

Only you see your depth score. It's a personal compass, not a public ranking. There's no "depth leaderboard." The moment you can compare, it becomes competitive. We prevent that.

Rewards Listening

Reading without responding doesn't hurt your score. Asking genuine questions raises it. The system values curiosity and engagement over output volume.

// PRINCIPLES IN PRACTICE

How It's Built

Architecture that enforces our values. Not policies. Code.

Chronological by Design

There is no recommendation engine to build. The feed is chronological from your connections. Period. No "because you liked" suggestions. No trending topics. You see what people you chose to follow actually posted.

Ephemeral by Default

Campfire messages are stored in memory, not on disk. When the 12 hours expire, the data is zeroed. Not "deleted" (recoverable). Zeroed (gone). We can't produce what we don't have.

No Tracking Infrastructure

We don't have analytics on individual users. No session tracking. No click maps. No "engagement time" metrics. We literally cannot A/B test re-engagement because we don't track engagement.

Open Data Format

Export everything you've posted in JSON, Markdown, or ActivityPub format. Take your content anywhere. We don't hold your data hostage. If a better BackRoad appears, leave freely.

Self-Hostable

Run your own BackRoad instance. Federate with the main network or stay private. Your community, your rules, your infrastructure. We publish the code.

Moderation Without Surveillance

Community-driven moderation through campfire hosts and connection-based trust. No automated content scanning. No shadow bans. Transparent community standards enforced by humans.
